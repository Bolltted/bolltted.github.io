Welcome

I'm Keylan Tarasoff, bringing a strong foundation in Computer Engineering combined with extensive experience as a commercial helicopter pilot. I hold certifications from Transport Canada (CPL H (TC)) and the Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines (CPL H (CAAP)), with expertise in flying various helicopter models, including the Hughes 500 (HU50) and Bell 206 (BH06).

My passion lies at the intersection of technology and aviation, where I focus on driving innovation and efficiency. Leveraging my computer engineering background and White hat practices honed from an early age; I develop software and enhance security for clients to optimize flow of operations, privacy, and integrate cutting-edge technology. My extensive hands-on experience as a commercial pilot provides a unique perspective when tackling these challenges.
